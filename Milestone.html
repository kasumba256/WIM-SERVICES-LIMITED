Our Journey

Key Milestones

2013
WIM Services is Born

Registered in Kampala on May 20, 2013. First contracts with World Vision Uganda for VIP latrines and classroom blocks.

2013
Oil & Gas Space

Entered the oil and gas sector with Total E&P Uganda civil maintenance works — first contract worth over UGX 500m.

2017
ISO 9001 Certification

Recognized by the International Organization for Standardization, validating our commitment to quality and global standards.

2018
First Road Project

Expanded into road development, beginning a trajectory that would become our flagship service.

2020
First Asphalt Road

Completed first asphalt road in Namulonge for the National Agricultural Research Organization (NARO).

2023
State House Projects

Presidential Initiative for construction of industrial hubs in Kween and Napak, Karamoja sub-region.

Today
Growing Strong

Annual turnover exceeds UGX 15B with projects spanning road construction, building, and water engineering across Uganda.
