About Us

A Legacy of Purpose &
Engineering Excellence

WIM Services Limited is a 12-year-old Ugandan-registered multi-disciplinary engineering company with proven capabilities in road construction, building construction, and water engineering.

Established by two engineering specialists with over 15 years of experience each, we have built a reputation for reliable engineering services from feasibility studies and design to construction management and maintenance. Our annual turnover has grown from UGX 500m to over UGX 15b today.

Our Mission
High quality services in roads, water supply and building engineering in a timely, cost-effective, healthy and safe environment.
Our Vision
To be a leading provider of civil and building engineering services that meet our customers' expectations.
